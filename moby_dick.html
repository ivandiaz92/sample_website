Moby-Dick (or, The Whale)

The Softcoverpublishing platafom was designed mainly for ebooks like the Ruby on Rails Tutorial book, and Learn Enough to be Dangerous. But it´s also good for making more traditional books, such as the novel Moby Dick by Herman Melville (Sometimes written as Moby Dick). We present below a short and affectionately irreverent book report on this classic of American Literature.

Moby Dick: A Classic Tale of the Sea

Moby Dick By Herman Melville, begins with this immortal words:

Call me Ishmael. Some years ago-never mind how long precisely- having little or no money in my purse, and nothing particular to interest me on shore, I though I would sail about a little and see the watery part of the world. It is a way I of driving off the spleen and regulating the circulation.

After drivinf of his spleen (which can´t be good for you). Ishmael then goes in much of the same vain for aproximately one jillion pages. The only thing bigger than Moby Dick(who-spoiler alert!-is a giant whale) is the book itself.

Our top 3 favorite things of Moby Dick

  1. Vengeful Wahle
  2. Salty Sailors
  3. The names 'Queequeg' and 'Starbuck'

Other things about Moby

  • Chapter after chapter (after chapter) of meticulous detail about whaling.
  • The story pretty much happened in real life
  • Mad sea captains are fun
